      Description

      Draughts, sometimes known as Checkers Legend,
      is a category of two-player strategic board games that call for obligatory
      captures by leaping over the pieces of the opposition.
      Alquerque is the basis for checkers.
      A popular board game that dates back to around 3000 BC is checkers.
      Although incredibly easy, it is a lot of fun! In England,
      checkers is referred to as draughts, and it has several regional variants.
      The game is played on an 8x8 chess-like board with checkerboard pattern.
      Twelve pieces are put on the board's darkest squares nearest to each player at the beginning.
