      Merge Cakes

      Description

      The casual merging game Merge Cakes was created by Ravalmatic.
      Based on the fundamental idea that matching two cakes of the same kind will result in an enhanced cake,
      this addicting game builds up and engulfs you in a wave of satisfaction.
      You'll discover unexpected presents, paid advertisements to enhance those gifts,
      enhanced cakes that may be purchased, fresh, exotic recipes, hidden bakeries, and much more.
      In addition to all of this, the game continues to run even while you are offline,
      ensuring that awards are always waiting for you when you come back.
      Are you prepared for this tasty adventure?
